Who I Am

About Me

I'm Sushrut — a Program Manager at Apple, based in Bangalore, originally from Nagpur. I did my BTech and MTech from IIT Madras, and over the past five years I've grown into roles where structured thinking and calm problem-solving matter most. I enjoy the work, but I don't let it define me entirely.

Outside the office, I play the harmonium — mostly classical and devotional pieces — and that's been a quiet constant in my life. I stay active through badminton and the gym, enjoy travelling when I can, and value conversations that actually go somewhere. Theater has been another long-standing interest, though more as an appreciator than a performer.

"I prefer a steady, grounded life over a flashy one — someone who shows up consistently for the people who matter."

Family is close to my heart. My parents are in Nagpur, my brother in Pune, and moving to Bangalore has been a deliberate step — building something here, not just passing through. My extended family is warm and closely knit, and music runs through both sides of it.
